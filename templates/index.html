What is SKIVI?

SKIVI is a cutting-edge tool designed to analyze skin conditions using advanced machine learning algorithms. Simply upload a picture of your skin condition, and our tool will identify the issue, assess its severity, and provide tailored precautions and treatment options.

Our goal is to empower individuals with knowledge about their skin health, offering insights and guidance that can lead to better care and treatment decisions. With SKIVI, understanding your skin condition has never been easier.

How SKIVI Works

Once you upload your image, SKIVI processes it using trained models that recognize various skin conditions. Our AI-driven analysis ensures quick and reliable results, helping you understand your skin health better.

The tool analyzes features such as texture, color variations, and patterns within the skin image to provide an accurate diagnosis. Users receive personalized insights that can aid in their treatment journey.
